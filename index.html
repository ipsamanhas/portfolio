Roles and experiences

Royal Bank of Canada (RBC) Borealis

Machine Learning Mentee Mar 2026 – May 2026

Let’s SOLVE it is a RBC Borealis mentorship program that provides undergraduate students from diverse backgrounds with mentorship, contacts, training and guidance they would need to make their project a reality.

  • Worked in a team of 5 to develop a computer vision triage tool called SkinBuddy
  • Guided by RBC mentors and industry experts, we came up with the idea of building something that utilizes AI in Healthcare.
  • Developed the app in Flutter for both iOS and Android.

Alberta Machine Intelligence Institute (Amii)

Machine Learning Fellow May 2025 – June 2025

The AI4Good Lab is a 7-week program that runs in collaboration with 3 AI institutes in Canada. It equips women and gender diverse people with the skills to build their own machine learning projects, and gives them a platform to break into tech.

  • Worked in a team of 5 to develop a data-driven neighbourhood recommendation system called resiSense.
  • Got hands-on experience with supervised and unsupervised learning, reinforcement learning, and NLP.
  • Presented our final project to a panel of industry experts and mentors on Demo Day at Mila, Montréal.
